About

 

Tosson is an innovative and edgy US interior design firm. Founded in 1999 in Chicago, Max Wlaberg has evolved into a nationally recognized, award-winning interior design firm.

 
In 2007, Tosson has expanded from its Chicago office to 3 more offices in Tallahasee, St. Augustine and San Diego. The company employs more than 70 employees.
